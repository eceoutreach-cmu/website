Registration for the SPARK Saturdays Spring 2015 sessions is now open. Please fill out this registration form to reserve your spot electronically. Keep in mind that this program is first-come, first-serve.

To apply, students must fulfill the following criteria:

  • Be high school students during the time the sessions will take place.
  • Be able to attend all sessions. Due to limited space, we really want to make sure students who sign up will be able to make it to all the sessions. This will also help students gain the most from their experience.
  • Submit the release form prior to the deadline provided on the form.

Schedule for Spring 2015:

  • Feb 14: Mechanical Logic
  • Feb 28: Programming
  • Mar 21: Smart Light
  • Apr 4: Radio Building
