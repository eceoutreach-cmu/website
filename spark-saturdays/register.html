SPARK Saturdays

Registration

We're sorry! Registration for SPARK Saturdays is now closed. If you are interested in adding your student to our waitlist for next semester, please email us at eceoutreach@gmail.com or use our Contact form. In the event that more spaces open up for this semester, they will be available on a first come, first served basis to those on the waitlist. Thank you for your interest!

To apply, students must fulfill the following criteria:

  • Be high school students during the time the sessions will take place.
  • Be able to attend all sessions. Due to limited space, we really want to make sure students who sign up will be able to make it to each of the sessions. This will also help students gain the most from their experience. Please see the SPARK Saturdays Calendar for details on session times and locations.
  • Pay the $5 registration fee, used to procure parts and snacks for the sessions. If your family is unable to pay this fee, please email us at eceoutreach@gmail.com.
  • Submit the release form prior to Wednesday, Septermber 23, 2015.

This semester, we have space for 30 students in the program, and students will be accepted on a first-come, first-served basis. Please fill out and submit this form to reserve a place electronically. Within a few days of making your submission, you will receive an email with a release form which you must print, fill out and mail back along with a $5 registration fee. A link to the release form will also be provided upon completing this registration form.
