We need volunteers for a whole host of tasks. Volunteers are the reason our program has been so successful so far. Whether you are an undergraduate student, a graduate student or a professor, we would love to have you participate in our program. Get in touch with us using our Contact Us page to express your interest in becoming a volunteer.

We need volunteers to:

  • Help create materials for sessions.
  • Contact schools and students to generate interest for this program.
  • Run the sessions.
  • Help us expand our program to more students of more ages.
  • Keep up the website and help with on-campus publicity.
  • Join the executive board to lead the program.
  • Interface with potential sponsors.
  • If you are a current volunteer wishing to fill our your clearance forms, they can be found here, along with appropriate instructions.
