Snap Circuits

Registration

Register your student for the Spring 2016 iteration of our Snap Circuits program, taking place Saturday, February 20, 2016! To apply, students must fulfill the following criteria:

  • Be in middle school during the time the sessions will take place.
  • Submit the release form by Wednesday, February 17, 2016.

This semester, we have space for 20 students in the program, and students will be accepted on a first-come, first-served basis. Please fill out and submit this form to reserve a place electronically. Within a few days of making your submission, you will receive an email with a release form which you must print, fill out, and mail back. A link to the release form will also be provided upon completing this registration form.

Registration closes on Friday, February 12, 2016 at 11:59pm, or when we have reached max capacity.
